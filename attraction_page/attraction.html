Tanjung Puting National Park

相關簡介:

Established in 1982, Tanjung Puting National Park is a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve and a haven for endangered wildlife like orangutans, proboscis monkeys, and sun bears. Explore the park's diverse ecosystems, ranging from peat swamp forests to pristine rivers teeming with aquatic life.

票價:

NTD 100 for adults, with additional fees for specific activities like guided tours and boat trips

Derawan Islands

相關簡介:

Escape to paradise on this archipelago of pristine islands boasting crystal-clear turquoise waters, idyllic beaches fringed with swaying palm trees, and a vibrant coral reef ecosystem. Dive into a world of tranquility and natural beauty on the Derawan Islands. Relax on pristine beaches, go snorkeling or diving amidst stunning coral reefs teeming with colorful fish, and explore hidden lagoons and mangrove forests. The islands are also home to a unique culture with traditional villages and friendly locals.

票價:

Varies depending on the specific island and chosen activities. Some islands like Kakaban have a conservation fee of around IDR 150,000 (around USD $10.50) per person.

Sebangau National Park

相關簡介:

Explore the intricate network of blackwater rivers snaking through the park's dense rainforests. Spot orangutans, proboscis monkeys, and a diverse array of birds, insects, and reptiles. Sebangau National Park offers a truly off-the-beaten-path adventure for nature enthusiasts.

票價:

NTD 100 for adults, with additional fees for specific activities like guided tours and boat trips
